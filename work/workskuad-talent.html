Reimagining Talent Acquisition: A Complete Recruitment Ecosystem

Designing a dual-sided recruitment ecosystem that serves both hiring teams and job seekers.

The Challenge

The recruitment landscape had a fundamental problem: companies couldn't efficiently find quality talent, while skilled professionals faced opaque, frustrating application processes. The goal was to design a platform to solve both sides of this equation.

Insight

Transparency transforms recruitment. When both sides have clear visibility—candidates into their application status, companies into their pipeline—everyone makes better decisions and has more positive experiences.

Research & Pain Points

Companies Needed:

  • Centralized pipeline management across all open roles.
  • Real-time visibility into hiring progress.
  • Streamlined candidate communication.
  • Data-driven decision-making tools.

Candidates Wanted:

  • Transparent application tracking.
  • Professional, respectful communication.
  • Fair opportunity to demonstrate their skills.
  • Clear understanding of where they stand in the process.
The Solution

For Companies: Command & Control

  • Recruitment Dashboard: Pipeline visualization showing candidate progression across all hiring stages.
  • AI-Assisted Job Posting: Smart skills suggestions and compensation benchmarking.
  • Profile-Rich Candidate Cards: Featuring portfolios, assessments, and video introductions.

For Candidates: Transparency & Empowerment

  • Guided Profile Creation: Progressive disclosure to build comprehensive profiles without overwhelming users.
  • Three-Stage Application Process: Pre-qualification questions, skills assessments, and built-in video interviews.
  • Real-time Status Tracking: Keeping candidates informed throughout the entire process.
  • Community Features: Referral system with transparent reward tracking and professional networking.
Key Design Decisions
  • Mobile-First for Candidates: Touch-friendly interfaces recognizing that most applications happen on phones.
  • Desktop-Optimized for Companies: Complex data visualization and multi-column layouts for comprehensive pipeline management.
  • Trust Through Design: Professional color palette and consistent patterns for companies; welcoming, approachable design with progress indicators for candidates.
Impact

Companies: Increased time reviewing individual candidates, improved feedback quality, and data-driven job posting refinement.

Candidates: Higher application completion rates, stronger community engagement, and increased referrals.

Overall Result: A positive feedback loop where better candidate experiences attract higher-quality applicants, leading to more successful hires.

Design Screens Summary
  • Recruitment Dashboard: Intuitive talent dashboard for monitoring active job openings, candidate pools, and pipeline stages with metrics like time-to-hire.
  • AI Job Posting Workflow: Guides companies through job creation with templates and real-time previews, reducing posting time by 60%.
  • Candidate Profile View: Comprehensive system showcasing expertise, experience timelines, availability, and expected rate ranges.
  • Integrated Job Board: Seamless application tracking and advanced filtering that increased completion rates by 45%.
Future Evolution
  • Enhanced Matching Intelligence: Skills-based algorithms and cultural fit assessments.
  • Deeper Integration: Calendar syncing for scheduling and project-based hiring tools.
  • Global Expansion: Multi-language support and regional compliance localization.
Personal Reflection

Success in marketplace design comes from building a single ecosystem where both sides thrive together. The biggest challenge was resisting the urge to build everything at once, focusing instead on core interactions first.

Key learning: Great B2B design requires understanding the real people behind company accounts.
