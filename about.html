About

I make products that earn trust.

I've spent 5+ years working at the intersection of complex systems and everyday users — designing products in fintech, SaaS, HR, and B2C that reduce friction and drive measurable outcomes. I don't just make things look good. I make them work.

What I believe
01

Ship beats perfect.

A good product in users' hands beats a perfect design in Figma. I optimise for learning velocity, not pixel perfection.

02

Outcomes over outputs.

Screens don't create value. Behaviour change does. I measure success by what users do, not what they see.

03

Constraints are creative fuel.

The best design work I've done happened under real pressure — tight timelines, technical limits, and ruthless scope.

04

Design is a team sport.

I do my best work alongside engineers, PMs, and researchers — not in isolation. Collaboration isn't a soft skill, it's a design tool.
